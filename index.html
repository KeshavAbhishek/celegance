ABOUT
CELEGANCE

Celegance is not just a series of events; it’s an experience that goes far beyond that. The anticipation leading up to the marquee event of our college is something that must be seen and believed. Every year, over 6,000 students from various branches and streams come together to celebrate and enjoy this wonderful array of cultural events. It provides students with an opportunity to interact, bond, and create memories through fun games and activities, building connections that will be cherished for a lifetime.

This two-day extravaganza is packed with ecstatic performances, adrenaline-pumping competitions, and joyful laughter echoing all around. The event’s buildup is nothing short of spectacular, with teasers and trailers flooding social media, flash mobs introducing events across campus, and organizing teams working tirelessly to complete preparations. All this hard work and enthusiasm culminates under a star-lit sky and a vibrant stage, making these two days truly unforgettable. In the end, it can be said that Celegance is not just an event to experience but a celebration to live and cherish.
